Bookkeeping Services

As a business owner, you may know the importance of reliable accounting and bookkeeping. If you don't stay on top of your finances, things can get out of hand every quickly. Lebam Financial Services is certified in QuickBooks with a vast amount of experience in the accounting and bookkeeping industry. By providing daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly or even year-end bookkeeping for small to medium-sized businesses, we give business owners an affordable and efficient way to receive up-to-date and accurate bookkeeping records. Enjoy full-service bookkeeping with timely and accurate services. Daily or weekly bookkeeping would include aspects such as Accounts Receivable Invoicing, Accounts Payable Issue cheques, bank deposits, recording vendor invoices, inventory tracking, as well as time and expenses tracking. Monthly or qarterly bookkeeping includes aspects such as bank, credit card, or inventory reconcillation, sales tax returns (including PST, GST, HST), periodic adjusting entries, and financial statements. For year-end bookkeeping, we prepare working papers for accountants and auditors accruals, inventory adjustments, capital asset schedules and year-end financial statements. The team at Lebam Financial Services strives to always meet deadlines and other important dates to provide you with accurate statements that inform you of how well your business is doing, and which financial decisions would benefit your business the best.
